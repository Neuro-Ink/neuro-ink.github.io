Company Overview

NeuroInk, founded in 2021 and based in Bengaluru, India, is an innovative technology company specializing in smart writing instruments. With a mission to revolutionize the way people capture and share ideas, NeuroInk combines traditional writing with advanced digital technology, creating a unique blend of functionality and creativity.

Product Innovation

NeuroInk's flagship product is a groundbreaking pen that not only writes but also extracts vector graphics of the written data into a compact USB drive. Utilizing simple pressure sensors and a tiny chip, the pen seamlessly transfers files via Bluetooth, making it an essential tool for professionals, students, and creatives alike. This innovative approach allows users to digitize their handwritten notes effortlessly, enhancing productivity and collaboration in various fields.

Made in India

Proudly manufactured in India, NeuroInk emphasizes quality and craftsmanship in every product. The company is committed to supporting local suppliers and fostering a sustainable manufacturing process, contributing to the "Make in India" initiative. By prioritizing local resources, NeuroInk not only boosts the economy but also ensures that its products meet the highest standards of excellence.

Vision and Mission

NeuroInk aims to empower individuals by bridging the gap between analog and digital worlds. The company envisions a future where writing is not just a means of communication but also a powerful tool for creativity and collaboration. Through continuous innovation and a focus on user experience, NeuroInk strives to enhance productivity and inspire new ways of thinking, ultimately transforming how people interact with their ideas.
